Dobrodošli

The luggage is all packed up. On August 25 2022 I boarded the plane from Vienna International Airport to Istanbul. From there I boarded the direct plane to Taiwan, my current refuge. Yet, not everything went as smooth as I expected. The guy standing in front of the gate insisted that I give him a document that I did not need. However, he warned me I might not be able to board the plane. The start was not so pleasant, let alone the fact the airplane was delayed by two hours.

After the abysmal 11 hours in plane I've arrived on a tobacco-shaped island. Taiwan, literally the Terrace Gulf. Ilha Formossa.

After registering myself in the Quarantine Health Check I boarded the quarantine taxi, awaiting Taichung and Tunghai University.

But alas, now I am left to spend a whole week in quarantine:

2022 Ondřej Pavlík. Všechna práva vyhrazena.
